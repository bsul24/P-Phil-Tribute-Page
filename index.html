Punxsutawney Phil

The only groundhog with any answers

Punxsutawney Phil ponders what he sees before him, the fate of humanity (for the next six weeks) depending on him, as a member of the Inner Council excitedly awaits his prophecies.

The following are a series of events in the storied groundhog's life:

  • 1886 - Born in Punxsutawney, Pennsylvania
  • 1887 - Makes his first weather prediction, where he predicted a long winter
  • 1889 - Fails to record a prediction for the first time (reasons unknown)
  • 1890 - Makes his first early spring prediction
  • 1893 - Turns seven years old, surpassing the average groundhog lifespan (six years)
  • 1903 - Predicts a long winter, starting a run of 31 straight long winter predictions, the longest such streak
  • 1942 - Unable to make an accurate prediction, as war clouds had blacked out part of the shadow
  • 1943 - Did not make an appearance, likely related to the continuance of war
  • 1986 - Turns 100 years old
  • 1993 - Makes his first big screen appearance in the Bill Murray blockbuster movie Groundhog Day
  • 1995 - Following the success of Groundhog Day, he made an appearance on The Oprah Winfrey Show
  • 2021 - Due to the global COVID-19 pandemic, for the first time he made his prediction without a crowd present
  • 2022 - Most recently predicted a long winter
